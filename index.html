I’m Juan Carlos, a Senior ML Engineer at Marvik. I build applied machine learning and generative AI systems, with a focus on moving research ideas into products people can use.

I studied music before working in AI, and I still think a lot about creative tools: what happens when machines can compose, generate, search, and collaborate with us.

The thread through my work is ML engineering at the intersection of music, creative AI, and production systems.
